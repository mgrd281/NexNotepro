## App Description

NexNote is not another notes app. It's a next-generation thinking system designed for people who value clarity, creativity, and calm.

Capture fleeting ideas in Quick Capture mode. Dive deep with Deep Thinking mode. Reflect on your day with Reflection mode. Turn thoughts into action with Task-Oriented mode. Every thought gets the space it deserves.

**THOUGHT MODES**
Each note adapts to how you're thinking. Choose from Idea, Deep Thinking, Quick Capture, Reflection, or Task-Oriented — and watch the interface respond to your mindset.

**MIND LAYERS**
Notes aren't flat. Add sub-thoughts, insights, and action items as layers within any note. Your ideas have depth — NexNote respects that.

**MEMORY SPACES**
Forget boring folders. Organize your thoughts into Memory Spaces — Work, Personal, Ideas Lab, and Life Journal — each with its own identity and feel.

**INSIGHT ENGINE**
NexNote detects decisions, tasks, ideas, and follow-ups hiding in your notes. See patterns in your thinking you never noticed before.

**FOCUS WRITING MODE**
Full-screen, zero-distraction writing with beautiful serif typography. Just you and your thoughts.

**EMOTIONAL TAGGING**
Tag your notes with how you feel — Stressed, Inspired, Focused, or Confused. The app subtly adapts to your emotional state.

**SMART TIMELINE**
See your thoughts unfold over time in a beautiful visual timeline. Your thinking journey, beautifully mapped.

**AI THINKING TOOLS**
Suggest titles, summarize long thoughts, and extract tasks — all with a single tap.

## Design Decisions

### Why Plus Jakarta Sans?
Geometric, modern, highly legible — it signals "tech-forward" while remaining warm and approachable. Used across all UI elements for consistency.

### Why Source Serif 4 for Focus Mode?
Serif fonts reduce cognitive load in long-form writing. Source Serif 4 is elegant and comfortable for extended reading and writing sessions.

### Why a left-border accent on cards?
A 3px colored left border instantly communicates the Thought Mode without adding visual noise. It's a subtle but powerful information-dense design choice.

### Why soft indigo-to-lavender gradient?
Indigo conveys intelligence and focus. Lavender adds warmth and calm. Together they position NexNote as both powerful and emotionally comfortable — the core brand promise.

### Why no folders?
Folders are a filing-cabinet metaphor from the 1980s. Memory Spaces are emotionally resonant, context-aware containers that feel more human. Users think in contexts, not hierarchies.

### Why emotional tagging?
Traditional note apps ignore how the user feels. Emotional context changes how thoughts should be reviewed later. A stressed note from Monday reads differently on Friday — the tag preserves that context.

### Why the Insight Engine?
Most valuable thoughts are buried in long notes. The Insight Engine surfaces decisions, tasks, and follow-ups automatically — turning passive notes into active intelligence.
